Our Services

Nowadays, In our country traffic jam is a mysterious thing. General mass suffer a lot for traffic jam. There are many purposes for traffic jam. One of them is the manual tolling system in bridges, flyovers, express-way etc. If we will build a system that will help these vehicle to pre-pay all of these toll via online then this problem may be disappeared. We also have a purpose to categorize all of these vehicles. We create a database to all of these traffic which are available on these certain routes. When a vehicle owner pay its vehicle taxes then we check the information about vehicles fitness, license validity, driving license. If any of these information will negative then we don’t give the permission to cross this. That’s help us to avoid road accident and build up discipline transportation system in Bangladesh.

1. Firstly register the vehicles with all details information. These information are stored in our database.
